About Us

The NUNO Project's mission is to break generational trauma through the POWER of love, support and encouragement. While also bringing awareness to the dangers of Fentanyl and Opioid use.

We aim to be the voice of the voiceless making a difference for the coming generations by providing drug education, mentorship, and resources to youth as well as families.

As a non-profit organization we heavily rely on public donations. This initiative can make a significant impact on people's lives and create a better future for our communities.

The NUNO Project operates without a physical office to maintain maximum contribution back to the community.

Thank you for supporting the NUNO Project!
