Frequently Asked Questions

What is the NUNO project?

The NUNO Project is a nonprofit organization dedicated to improving the lives of children in need.

How can I donate to the NUNO project?

You can donate to The NUNO Project by clicking on the 'Donate' button on our website or by sending a check to our mailing address.

What programs does the NUNO project offer?

The NUNO Project offers a variety of programs, including after-school programs, summer camps, and mentoring programs.
